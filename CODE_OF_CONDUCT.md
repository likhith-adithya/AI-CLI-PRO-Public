# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community and its projects will be treated with respect, regardless of age, body size, caste, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity, or sexual orientation.

We are opposed to all form of harassment, discrimination and bullying. We are committed to building a community that is:

- **Inclusive**: We welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities
- **Respectful**: We treat all members with courtesy and respect
- **Safe**: We maintain a harassment-free environment
- **Constructive**: We focus on solutions, not blame
- **Honest**: We communicate truthfully and transparently

## Our Standards

### ✅ Examples of Behavior That Contribute to Our Community

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being patient and understanding with new contributors
- Acknowledging and celebrating others' contributions

### ❌ Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment of any kind (verbal, physical, written, or online)
- Discriminatory jokes and language
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Violence, threats of violence, or violent language
- Spam or off-topic promotional content
- Deliberate misinformation or spreading false information
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Document the issue** - Note the date, time, and details
2. **Report privately** - Contact the maintainers at: likhith.adithya@gmail.com
3. **Include context** - Provide links and relevant information
4. **Be safe** - If you feel unsafe, prioritize your safety first

**You are not obligated to confront the person directly.** We handle all reports confidentially.

### Consequences

Community leaders will determine consequences for violations. Actions may include:

1. **Warning**: Private message explaining the violation
2. **Temporary Ban**: Removal from discussions for 7-30 days
3. **Permanent Ban**: Removal from the community

All enforcement decisions are made fairly and consistently.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all community spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and discussions
- Pull request comments
- Code review feedback
- Email communications
- Any official community events
- Social media discussions about the project
